Mist Engine HUD

A simple always-open, never-in-the-way HUD for City of Mist and Otherscape game systems. It allows players to quickly select tags and statuses and do the Moves rolls, with no questions or menus to answer. Just select what you need and roll using the Moves in the Hotbar. Fast and streamlined like any action-packed game should be!

FoundryVTT Module

Tactical Map Switcher

A must-have for investigative games like City of Mist, Call of Cthulhu, and any of your thrilling conspiracy adventures. The Investigation Board module allows everyone at the table to collaborate interactively, sharing clues and discoveries directly within the scene. It's perfect for piecing together evidence and unraveling mysteries—whether solving a criminal case or uncovering supernatural secrets.

Soundscape Adventure

Soundscape Adventure is a powerful and intuitive module designed to enrich the role-playing sessions with immersive audio experiences. Seamlessly integrated into FoundryVTT, it enables Game Masters to effortlessly synchronize ambient sounds and dynamic effects with the unfolding story. Transform the virtual tabletop games into captivating auditory journeys, bringing adventures vividly to life for all.

Tactical Map Switcher

Foundry VTT allows for countless memorable moments, from epic tactical battles to intense role-playing negotiations in Theater of the Mind. To make life easier for Game Masters, we've developed a module enabling effortless switching between narrative scenes and tactical maps within the active scene. This module provides seamless transitions, significantly enhancing your gaming experience—particularly valuable if you prefer Theater of the Mind but still want tactical precision during combat encounters.

About Us

Hello! We’re a passionate group of TTRPG players and Game Masters, immersed in this amazing hobby since the '90s. But our enthusiasm doesn't stop at the gaming table—we’re also seasoned tech professionals who've spent more than a decade bringing innovation to life, from vibrant startups to leading global tech companies.

By merging our love for tabletop adventures with our extensive technology expertise, we've committed ourselves to creating immersive, delightful experiences tailored specifically for Virtual Tabletops (VTTs). Whether you're seeking innovative modules, captivating game systems, or custom-built tools to elevate your gaming nights, we're dedicated to making your VTT sessions unforgettable.

Let us help transform your gaming dreams into reality—and deliver happiness straight to your virtual tabletop.
